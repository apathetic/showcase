About

AutoCompleter is a light wieght stand alone module can be used for filtering data or auto completion of user input. It work on desktop, phone and tablet.

Concept

AutoCompleter caches filterable data in an array, and each data key matches their markup order. By filtering the cached array to locate what markup to hide or show and reludce the amount markup to append to DOM.

Compatibility

  • IE9+
  • Chrome, Opera, safari
  • iOS 6+
